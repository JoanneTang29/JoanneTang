Chain Reaction

  • Objective : Two players compete to guess words that connect to the word above and below. Each word is part of a two-word term.
  • Example: Final four, four-square, square dance, dance party.
  • Players start with 500 points.
  • Player 1 will wager 50, 100, or 200 points.
  • Then select a word in the chain to guess.
  • Guess the word by typing your answer and pressing enter.
    • If the guess is correct, points wagered will add to total score.
    • If the guess is incorrect, points wagered will subtract from total score.
  • Player 1's turn is complete and player 2 will wager points and guess a word in the chain.
  • How to Win : Player with the most points at the end of round 3 WINS.
