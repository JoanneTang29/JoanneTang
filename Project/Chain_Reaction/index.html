Chain Reaction

  • Objective : Two players compete to guess words that connect to each other above and below in some way. All connected words make up a chain.
  • Players start with 500 points
  • First player wagers 50, 100, or 200 points.
  • Then, will select a word to guess.
  • If the guess is correct, you double the points you wagered.
  • If the guess is incorrect, you lose the points you wagered.
  • Player 2 will guess next.
  • Player with the most points at the end of round 3 WINS.
